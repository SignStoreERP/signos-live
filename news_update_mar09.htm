New Calculators Now Live!

We are excited to announce the launch of two new calculators in our retail estimation system: Basic ADA Signage and Engraved Nameplates & Inserts. These tools are designed to streamline the way you build and preview custom engraved orders.

🔵 Basic ADA Signage

🏷 Engraved Nameplates

🚀 How to use the new tools

1.

Open: Launch the portal and select either the ADA or Nameplate module.

2.

Configure: Input the dimensions, core material combinations, and required add-ons.

3.

Review: Check the live preview window and copy the approved retail price.

⚠️

See something, say something: If you spot a problem—like incorrect material colors, an inability to select a specific finish, or a layout issue—please let us know using the Submit Ticket button!

🟢 Available Material Options

The new tools feature a comprehensive list of standard materials pulled directly from our vendor catalogs:

Basic ADA Signage

  • 1/8" and 1/16" ADA Core
  • 3/16" Clear Acrylic Backers
  • Clear Raster Braille Beads
  • Tape & Stud Mounting

Nameplates & Inserts

  • 1/16" Front Engrave
  • Reverse Engrave Options
  • Custom Radius Corners
