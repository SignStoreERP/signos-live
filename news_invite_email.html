SignOS is our digital pricing portal designed to standardize quoting across the shop. This tool digitizes our physical pricing sheets for immediate, accurate retail pricing.

🚀 Quick Start Guide

  • 1. Login: Access the portal via your unique employee PIN.
  • 2. Select: Use the Dashboard to choose your material category.
  • 3. Quote: Input dimensions and finishing options for an instant price.

✨ Core Benefits

  • Centralized retail curves.
  • Real-time updates synchronized across all users.
  • Manufacturer compatibility safety rails built into every calculator.
