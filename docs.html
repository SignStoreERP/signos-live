Smart Production Update

Released March 2026

"Faster quoting, fewer errors."

We just pushed a major update to SignOS designed to make quoting faster, more visual, and perfectly aligned with our production standards. Here is what changed today based on your feedback:

🎨 Decals & Stickers

  • Replaced the old material dropdown with a fast, visual 8-button grid featuring icons for Vehicle Wraps, Window Perf, Drywall, and more.
  • Smart Laminate Pairings: Selecting a print material automatically filters and selects the correct, production-approved overlaminate.

📏 PVC Signs

  • Native support for sheet sizes up to 60" x 120" (5'x10').
  • Added bidirectional safety limits—the system will automatically adjust if you accidentally type a size larger than our max sheet bounds.

🧮 Banners, Wraps & Walls

  • Banners: Math has been updated to automatically round the width and height up to the nearest 12" increment before calculating square footage.
  • Wraps & Walls: Updated descriptions and names of vinyl/laminate options to be clearer and easier to navigate.
